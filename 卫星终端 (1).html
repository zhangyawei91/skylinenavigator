总结与展望

Summary and Outlook

车载卫星通信技术正朝着更安全、智慧化、集成化的方向发展，将在未来出行领域发挥更重要作用。随着技术的不断成熟和成本的降低，卫星通信有望成为智能汽车重要的基础设施。预计到2026年，将形成"3+N"市场格局（3类主流企业占据60%份额），卫星通信模块有望成为智能汽车第四核心部件（仅次于动力、智能、娱乐系统），在低空经济开放催生上百个新应用场景。企业重点关注技术融合、成本控制、用户体验优化等方面，推动车载卫星通信技术的持续发展与应用。

Vehicle satellite communication technology is developing towards safer, smarter, and more integrated solutions, playing an increasingly important role in future mobility. As technology matures and costs decrease, satellite communication is expected to become an important infrastructure for smart vehicles. By 2026, a "3+N" market pattern is expected to form (three mainstream enterprises occupying 60% of the market share), and satellite communication modules are expected to become the fourth core component of smart vehicles (after powertrain, intelligence, and entertainment systems), giving birth to hundreds of new application scenarios in the low-altitude economy. Enterprises focus on technology integration, cost control, user experience optimization, and other aspects to promote the continuous development and application of vehicle satellite communication technology.
